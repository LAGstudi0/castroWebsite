WHO ARE WE?

Step into Castro's Cafeteria, a lively spot wedged between Denbigh Castle and Ye Hole in Ye Wall. We, Borja Castro-Cabrera and Natasha, are here to infuse Liverpool with the spirit of Tenerife. Think tapas, Mechada, and a vibrant atmosphere that's pure sunshine!

At Castro's, Borja's culinary journey started from scratch, but now it's a full-blown flavor adventure. Our menu, born from Facetime sessions with family, boasts Tenerife's Mechada and moreâ€”fresh, delicious, and made with heaps of passion.

Castro's isn't just about food; it's a hub for connections. Our cozy space sparks conversations among strangers, turning them into friends over vibrant dishes. The energy spills into the nearby pubs, creating a fusion of English and Spanish camaraderie. Join us for unforgettable moments at Castro's Cafeteria! Your seat and a plate of goodness await!
